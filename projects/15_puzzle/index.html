Game Rules

The 15 Puzzle is a sliding puzzle consisting of 15 numbered tiles and one empty space. The objective is to arrange the tiles in ascending order from 1 to 15.

Rules:

  • You can move only adjacent tiles into the empty space.
  • The goal is to get the tiles arranged in sequence, left to right, top to bottom.
  • Try to solve it with the fewest moves possible!
